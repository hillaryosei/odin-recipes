Easy Air Fried Corn Ribs

These corn ribs are quite similar to elotes, the Mexican street corn, but each ear is split into four then thrown into the air fryer. Try this recipe out for yourself, and you're guaranteed to enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of corn cobs
  • 3 tablespoons Trader Joe's "Everything but the Elote Seasoning," divided
  • Non-stick cooking spray
  • 1/4 cup grated cotija cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Mexican crema fresca
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh cilantro, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 7 minutes.
  2. Stand corn cobs on its ends. With a sharp knife, cut each ear down the middle. Place corn cob halves flat on a cutting board and cut in half lengthwise. You will get a total of 4 'ribs' per ear of corn.
  3. Spray each corn 'rib' with non-stick spray. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of seasoning over the 16 corn 'ribs'.
  4. Air fry corn 'ribs' in batches for a total of 12 minutes. Remove from air fryer.
  5. Drizzle crema con sal over corn 'ribs' and sprinkle with cotija cheese and remaining seasoning. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
